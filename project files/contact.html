Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is PatternAI's classification system?

Our AI system achieves up to 98% accuracy in pattern classification, trained on millions of textile images across various pattern types including stripes, florals, geometric designs, and more.

What file formats do you support?

We support all major image formats including PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, BMP, and WebP. Maximum file size is 16MB per image.

Do you offer API access?

Yes, we provide RESTful API access for enterprise customers to integrate PatternAI directly into their existing workflows and systems.

What industries do you serve?

We serve fashion and apparel, textile manufacturing, interior design, retail, and any industry that works with patterned fabrics and textiles.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free account with limited usage to help you evaluate our service. You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime for unlimited access.
